The Challenge

Splitting bills shouldn't
require a spreadsheet

Every group dinner, trip, or shared expense turns into awkward math. ReceiptSplit eliminates the friction.

😤

The Problem

Splitting bills manually is slow, error-prone, and socially uncomfortable.

  • "Who had the extra appetizer?"
  • Calculator apps don't handle tax & tip per item
  • Venmo requests with wrong amounts
  • Group trips with dozens of shared receipts
✨

The Solution

AI-powered receipt scanning with intelligent item assignment and cent-perfect splitting.

  • OCR extracts every line item automatically
  • AI suggests who ordered what based on history
  • Tax & tip distributed proportionally per person
  • Multi-receipt events for group trips
